\section*{Advisory Expertise}
\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Converging contested calls:} When no one upstream has decided the direction and the
    direction is the hard part, I resolve the call, then build the team, architecture, and delegated
    ownership that keep it executing. The work is setting direction, not delegating a goal already set.
  \item \textbf{Turnaround under pressure:} A distinct trigger: crisis, not too many options. I enter
    broken, high-stakes systems and converge them on a working state.
  \item \textbf{Making technology legible:} I translate the capability and risk of AI into operating
    models that regulators and investors will scrutinise: proportionate guardrails that create value,
    not blanket caution. I can prepare a management team for regulatory questioning, having represented
    a bank at the UK Finance AI policy forum.
\end{itemize}
