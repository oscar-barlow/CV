\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Converging contested calls:} Where the direction itself is the hard part — no one upstream
    has decided it — I resolve the call, then build the team, architecture, and delegated ownership that
    keep it executing — setting the direction, not just delegating a goal already set.
  \item \textbf{Turnaround under pressure:} A distinct trigger — crisis, not too many options. I enter
    broken, high-stakes systems and converge them on a working state.
  \item \textbf{Making technical risk legible:} I turn ambiguous AI and data risk into operating models
    that regulators and investors will scrutinise — proportionate guardrails, not blanket caution — and
    can prepare a management team for regulatory questioning, having answered to the FCA and UK Finance
    AI policy forum myself.
\end{itemize}
